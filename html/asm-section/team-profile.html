  1. Team Name
    2. After much deliberation, we came to the decision that the new name for our group should be THKTemosure. The previous name, 2H, will no longer be used. This new name, in our opinion, is not only more meaningful but also more elegant. The acronym THKT stands for the initial letter of each member's name, and the word "emosure" refers to the process of measuring emotions; together, these two words convey the purpose of our software.
