Skills and Jobs
Our project involves developing an application to be able to perceive a person's emotional level. This can help bring harmony to your life and values. We believe that the following 4 positions are essential for the success of our project: Alternative Developer, Audit Staff, Psychology Expert, and Project Manager.
Alternative Developer:
- Proficient in coding.
- Know how to build a firewall system to secure the system and deal with hackers
- Fix application errors when there are vulnerabilities.
- Able to detect inadequacies in assigned work, present problems to superiors and propose solutions.

Audit Staff
- Controlling accounting activities, expenses, revenue and expenditure, debts
- Know how to prepare periodic financial statements
- Graduated with major in accounting from universities and colleges
- At least 2 years of general accounting experience

Psychology Expert
- Know how to solve problems and advise customers on the best solution possible
- Always adding ideas for solutions that are easier and less time consuming for customers to use

Project Manager
- A bachelor's degree in either business or computer science and years of professional experience in a relevant field  
- Manage the social media presence and assist with customer support 
- Establish working procedures and monitor closely to ensure software quality and project delivery on time 
- Take responsibility for the distribution of the budget
