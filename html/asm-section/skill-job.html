Skills and Jobs
Our project involves developing an application to be able to perceive a person's emotional level. This can help bring harmony to your life and values. To support the app better and faster, we need 1 developer to maintain, improve to make the app better.
Alternative Developers:
- Proficient in coding
- In addition to these skills, you must know how to build a firewall system to secure the system and deal with hackers and fix application errors when there are vulnerabilities.
- Able to detect inadequacies in assigned work, present problems to superiors and propose solutions.

Audit Staff
- Controlling accounting activities, expenses, revenue and expenditure, debts
- Know how to prepare periodic financial statements
- Graduated with major in accounting from universities and colleges
- At least 2 years of general accounting experience

Employees who know the career psychology of the application
- Know how to solve problems and advise customers on the best solution possible
- Always adding ideas for solutions that are easier and less time consuming for customers to use
